bargi dam, madhya pradesh

I visited this place in 2020

Bargi Dam is one of the first completed dams out of the chain of 30 major dams to be constructed on Narmada River in Madhya Pradesh, India. Here the Narmada river completely overlaps and submerges the Bargi River such that Bargi's independent existence is not noticeable.
